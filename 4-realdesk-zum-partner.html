RealDesk and Zum Rails Partner to Revolutionize Real Estate Payments

Our commitment to innovation shines through in this game-changing partnership.

Marc Sykes
Published on December 9, 2024

You may have seen that we were recently mentioned in a number of trade publications, including Fintech Finance, Pymnts, and The Green Sheet.

At RealDesk, we're on a mission to eliminate inefficiency in real estate, and replacing archaic payment methods with modern solutions has always been a key part of our vision. The ongoing Canada Post strike only serves to highlight how paper cheques simply no longer make sense when there are faster, cheaper, fully digital alternatives available.

I'm proud to see our partnership with Zūm Rails being recognized as a key element of our commitment to automating and streamlining the transaction life cycle, from lead to close. And we're just getting started — with additional payment features set for release this month, and more on the way in 2025 in our next-gen platform, RealDesk Pro.
