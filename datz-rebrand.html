Some Good News About the Future!

The next generation of Datz starts now.

Marc Sykes
Published on June 29, 2024

We are thrilled to share some exciting news with you! Effective July 1, Datz Solutions will be rebranded as RealDesk.AI. This change represents a significant milestone for us, as we transition to a new chapter with a stronger vision and a commitment to delivering exceptional value to our customers.

Why the Change?

RealDesk is the next generation of Datz. Our rebrand reflects our commitment to innovation, excellence, and a long-term vision to bring you enhanced features and capabilities over time. We are proudly Canadian-owned and Canadian-focused, and we couldn't think of a better day than Canada Day to launch our new identity.

What Does This Mean for You?

Right now, nothing will change in your current experience other than the name and logo. You will continue to use the same platform you always have. All Datz Solutions emails and URLs will stay the same. Over time, we will transition these to shiny new RealDesk addresses, but there will be a long overlap period to give everyone lots of time to get used to things.

What's Next?

While your immediate experience will remain the same, we are excited to share that this rebrand is designed to be much more than just a name change. Here’s what you can look forward to:

  • Innovative Solutions for the Future: With RealDesk, we are planning to introduce cutting-edge solutions that leverage the latest in proptech, fintech, and legal tech to bring you a more comprehensive, intuitive, and collaborative platform.

  • Improved and Simplified User Experience: We want to create a platform that’s easier to learn and master, getting rid of the long learning curves commonly found with other systems.

  • New Channels for Learning and Support: More documentation, guided workflows, help videos, and even mini-courses — however you learn best, whatever you need to know, there will be a channel that gives that to you.

  • Focus on Collaboration: As you know, Datz already gives agents, brokers, staff and clients unprecedented visibility into transaction status, financial statistics, and reporting. But there’s a lot more we can do with RealDesk, both within the brokerage and across the entire ecosystem.

We are excited about the future of RealDesk, and to renew our commitment to liberate real estate professionals from admin inefficiencies. We’re confident that this transition will lead to an even stronger partnership with you, our valued customers.

Finally, we want to express our gratitude for your continued trust and support. Thank you for being a valued part of our community.
