Company History

RealDesk originated from a pressing need within the real estate industry.

The real estate brokerage world lacked a comprehensive management system, leaving professionals reliant on a mishmash of inadequate, non-specialized tools that didn't seamlessly integrate.

Despite the high stakes and time-sensitive nature of these transactions, the existing technology proved remarkably inefficient, often leaving clients and stakeholders in the dark, susceptible to delays, and document mismanagement.

In response to this challenge, RealDesk was born as a holistic, interconnected solution to manage a real estate brokerage effectively. Built by brokers for brokers. This innovative platform captured the attention of other brokers seeking a similarly streamlined approach.

RealDesk's core mission is to empower all stakeholders involved in real estate transactions by providing them with a comprehensive, interconnected platform that revolutionizes efficiency and facilitates seamless information sharing. This system not only simplifies the entire transaction process but also ensures a user-friendly experience, making it accessible to individuals who may not be technology experts.

Agents and brokers value RealDesk for the peace of mind it offers, assuring them that they are always well-informed, leading to successful completion of their clients' deals without complications.

Since its establishment, RealDesk has achieved remarkable growth, doubling its customer base annually. The platform currently serves around 5,000 active users throughout Canada, with over 50,000 transactions processed, along with over $95 million in electronic funds transfer (EFT) and credit card payments.
